Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the PDF materials?

Once you open a specific course, click the "Materials" button at the top right. A panel will open showing all available PDFs for that course, which you can view or download instantly.

Does my progress save automatically?

Yes! Every time you open a video lecture, it is automatically marked as watched. Your progress is saved locally to your device, so you can track your total study hours and completion percentages on the dashboard.

What happens when a YouTube video is not loaded?

Sometimes videos take a moment to buffer. Please wait a bit, and a "Watch on YouTube" link will automatically be shown if the embed fails to load.

How do I watch the videos?

Simply click on any class card in the timeline. Please wait a bit for the video player to load. Once it opens, you can play, pause, and mark the class as completed.

Where are all the courses available and how do I find them?

You can find all available courses by clicking the "Courses" link in the top navigation bar, or by clicking the "Browse All Courses" button on the homepage to open the full course library.

When will new courses be updated?

There is no fixed schedule for new course updates. The platform administrator will add them automatically whenever the owner decides to release new content.
